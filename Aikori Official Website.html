Our Story

Welcome to Aikori: where heritage meets the hype

We are a boutique merchandise store dedicated to crafting high-quality, trendy collectibles that celebrate Malaysian identity.

We kicked things off with Leonor, our signature blindbox series inspired by the Harimau Malaya. By blending national pride with festive themes, we create 'mystery art' that resonates with the modern collector who values style and soul in equal measure.

"Designed with Pride. Crafted for Collectors."
